About APS Academy

A Unique Heritage School .

APS Academy started in 2023, presently one of the leading school of Mau. It has the best infrastructure in terms of highly techno savy Classrooms, well equipped library, well experienced staff, trained counselors and affiliated for 10+2 from Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi. APS Academy is a progressive, co-educational school, committed to providing best education for all its students. APS Academy has a qualified, experienced & dedicated team of teachers to impart quality education. We give importance to the quality education & enviroment in the school.
